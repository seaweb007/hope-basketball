Questions, answered

Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment does my child need to get started?

Just basketball shoes and comfortable athletic wear. We provide balls and all training equipment for every session.

Is there a tryout to join?

No tryout is required for Junior Foundations or Development Squad. Elite Prep involves a short skills assessment so coaches can place players at the right intensity level.

Can I book a free trial before committing?

Yes — every new player is welcome to one free trial session before registering, so you and your child can experience our coaching firsthand.

What is the refund or pause policy?

We understand plans change. Reach out to our team directly and we'll work with you on pausing or adjusting registration.

Do you help players get scouted or placed?

Yes — our Elite Prep track includes direct scouting exposure and placement support, building on our track record of 20+ collegiate and professional placements.
